*Single Asterisks*
is italic.

Use Asterisks for bullet lists:

* Item 1
* Item 2
* Item 3

Will become

  • Item 1
  • Item 2
  • Item 3

And similarily,

1. Item 1
2. Item 2
3. Item 3

Will become

  1. Item 1
  2. Item 2
  3. Item 3
