Resource App: Privacy Policy

The City of Hope Outreach Resource App is a directory of organizations in central Arkansas that offer various service ranging from financial assistance and food pantries to disability and rehabilitation services. We try to keep the information up to date. If you find a problem with a resource listed in the app, don't hesitate to report it by scrolling down to the bottom of a resource and tapping "Report Issue".

Our privacy policy is simple. This app is simply a directory, and we do not collect any information of any kind. If you report an issue with a resource, the only information we store is 1) the resource reported, 2) the type of feedback (either inaccurate details, confusing wording, or incorrect formatting), and 3) any comments entered before submitting.

To learn more about the City of Hope Outreach, visit https://coho58.org/
